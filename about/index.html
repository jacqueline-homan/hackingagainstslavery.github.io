About

The goal of hacking against slavery is to write free software for researchers, government agencies, non-profits, and communities interested in combatting slavery, or it's broader definition - human trafficking.

If you are interested in contributing to any or all of our projects, please send an email to hackingagainstslavery@gmail.com and we'll sign you up for our slack channel. Once you've been invited to join our slack channel is at https://hackingagainstslavery.slack.com Note: email will be checked once each day in the morning, so if you reach out in the afternoon expect a one day delay :)
