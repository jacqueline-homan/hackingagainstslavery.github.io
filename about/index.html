About

The goal of hacking against slavery is to write free software for researchers, government agencies, non-profits, and communities interested in combatting slavery, or it's broader definition - human trafficking.

If you are interested in contributing to any or all of our projects, please sign up for our [slack channel](https://hackingagainstslavery.slack.com) and email us at hackingagainstslavery at gmail dot com. This way we'll be sure you're coming. Note: email will be checked once each day in the morning, so if you reach out in the afternoon expect a one day delay :)
